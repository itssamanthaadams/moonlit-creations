About

Hi, I'm Samantha.

I'm a Software Engineering student, Front-End Developer, and aspiring UI/UX Designer based in Michigan.

I enjoy creating digital experiences that blend aesthetics with usability — from luxury brand websites to internal business platforms. My work is inspired by thoughtful design, beauty, nature, and the belief that technology should feel intuitive and human.

Moonlit Creations by Samantha is my space for building polished, intentional web experiences that feel clear, elegant, and memorable.

“Design should guide, not overwhelm.”

I believe the best digital experiences feel natural, organized, and emotionally considered.
